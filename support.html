Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account on the website?

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button located at the top right corner of the homepage.
Fill out the required information, including your name, email address, password and select what type of user you are(Example: General user/ Landlord).
Click "Sign Up Now" to complete the registration process.

How do I list my property?

To add your property, sign up as a Landlord and navigate to the "Explore Property" section. Fill in the necessary details by clicking "Add Property" button, and your property will be live on our platform.

How long does Booking take?

The booking process is quick and easy. Simply select the property you wish to book, and then click on "Book Now".

How can I contact the property owner?

Each property listing includes a "Book now" button. Click on "Book now" and the Landlord of the Property will get an email and contact with you.

What should I do if I encounter a problem?

If you encounter any issues, please reach out to our support team using the email support@propops.com.
